Department of Opthalmology

Overview

The Department of Ophthalmology shoulders multiple responsibilities: it functions as tertiary eye care center for the country, resource center for Primary Eye Care Program for Ministry of Health, as one of the core department for faculty of Postgraduate Medicine, KGUMBS. Therefore, it provides comprehensive and selected sub-specialty eye care ser- vices and outreach community eye services. The department is managed by a team of ophthalmologists, optometrists and ophthalmic assistants.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in opthalmology, pain management, and critical care in Bhutan and beyond.

Achievements

  • Construction of Gyalyum Kezang Choedon Wangchuck National Eye Centre at JDWNRH which is expected to complete in June 2019.
  • Surgical eye camp with Tarayana Foundation at Punakha
  • Participated in 2nd National RAAB Survey (Rapid Assessment of Avoidable Blindness) by Ministry of Health in technical collaboration with IAPB and fund- ing support by Lions International Club Foundation.
