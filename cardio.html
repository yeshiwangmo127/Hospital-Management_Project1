Department of Cardiology

Providing cardiac, intensive care, and pain management with compassion and innovation.

Overview

The CardiologyDepartment at Purepath plays a vital role in patient care by ensuring safe and effective care for surgeries, managing pain, and providing critical care services. Our expert team supports surgical procedures across all specialties with dedication and skill.

Vision

To provide exceptional heart care and become a leader in cardiovascular health through innovation, compassion, and excellence.

Mission

  • To deliver comprehensive and patient-centered cardiac services
  • Promote continuous education and training in cardiology.
  • Support advance cardiac care through education, research, and skilled healthcare

Core Values

  • Patient-Centered Care
  • Excellence & Safety
  • Compassion & Empathy
  • Integrity & Professionalism

Services Offered

  • Outpatient heart consultations
  • ECG, echocardiography, and stress tests
  • Emergency care for heart attacks
  • Heart failure management and rehabilitation
  • Preventive cardiology and lifestyle counseling
