Department of Anesthesiology

Providing Paediatrics, intensive care, and pain management with compassion and innovation.

Overview

The essence of paediatrics is to give service to those most deserving of a good life, with the deepest need, and the greatest vulnerability. The department of paediatrics at JDWNRH celebrates the work that we do every day to provide excellent clinical care to children below the age of twelve and also to train the next generation of paediatricians thereby improving the health and well-being of children and their families here in Bhutan. We have 74 inpatient beds in the department including 12 in the Intensive care unit. We also run the general paediatric OPD from Monday to Saturday and provide 24/7 inpatient and emergency services here at the hospital. In addition, we provide consultative services to all categories of health care workers all around the country on a 24/7 basis. We also provide technical guidance to various public health programs of the Ministry of Health.
